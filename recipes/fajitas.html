Fajitas

Easy Fajita recipe

Fajitas that don't make your stomach work

Ingredients

  • Wraps
  • Fake chicken of your choice
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Peppers
  • Salsa
  • Coriander
  • Mexican spice in a packet
  1. Chop onion and peppers and fry until golden
  2. Add mexican spice with a little water to prevent sticking to the pan
  3. Add fake chicken pieces and cook until done
  4. Add coriander, cook for a further 2 mins
  5. serve in wraps and add salsa or sauce of your choice
