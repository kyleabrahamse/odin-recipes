Stir Fry

A wholesome and quick stir fry

Don't break your wife's bowl or she'll be mad

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Brocoli
  • Cauliflower
  • Udon Noodles
  • Baby corn
  • Mange tout
  • Onion
  • Soy sauce
  • Syrup
  • Sriracha
  1. Chop onion, brocoli, cauliflower, and baby corn
  2. Fry above in a wok for 5mins
  3. Add tofu and scramble in wok
  4. Add soy sauce syrup and sriracha to taste
  5. Add Udon noodles and cook for 5 more mins
  6. Eat
