Curry

A wonderful vegan curry

This wonderful vegan curry will make you full and farty

Ingredients

  • 5 Potatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 can of Chickpeas
  • Purple raja curry spice
  • 1 cup Lentils
  • Lots of Coriander
  • 2 cloves of Garlic
  1. Boil potatoes and lentils
  2. Fry the onion until golden in separate big pot
  3. Add water and as much spice as necessary
  4. Add boiled potatoes and lentils
  5. Add can of chickpeas
  6. Simmer for 15 mins
  7. Add lots of Coriander
